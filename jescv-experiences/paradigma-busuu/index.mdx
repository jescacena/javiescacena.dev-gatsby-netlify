## Play and learn with Busuu

Busuu is actually a language from Cameroon in Africa, spoken by only 8 people!.
But as a company is a E-learnig platform for language learnings.
Based in UK , Paradigma had the choice of collaborate with them in stabilize their platform.

### Contributions
- **Frontend develop**. Creating new features in a startup pace.
- **Early PHP support**. My backend skills in PHP, help to maintain some features in early phases of the project.
- **Video chat platform recovering**. The E-learnig platform had a broken video chat platform. I helped to restore it.

### What i learned
- **Agile**. I was lucky to be in a well skilled people whom i learn about Scrum
- **Styling**. My CSS skills improved
- **Drag&Drop**. I learned a lot of transition/animations, drag'n drop in the develop of learning games
